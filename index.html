Exploring the IberoAmerican Collection

The present dashboard offers the possibility to analyze the correspondence preserved in the archives of the IIIC related to one of the literary collections the latter edited, namely, the Ibero-American Collection. More precisely, the dashboard results from the manual indexation of materials comprised in folder "Collection de Culture Ibéro-Américaine" (known as the IberoAmerican Collection) in the IIIC's funds (FR PUNES AG 01-IICI-F-VI).
